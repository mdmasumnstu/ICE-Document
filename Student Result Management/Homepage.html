Noakhali Science and Technology University (NSTU) is a leading public university in Bangladesh, established in 2006. Located in Sonapur, Noakhali, the university has emerged as a center of excellence in science and technology education, research, and innovation.
NSTU is committed to providing quality higher education and fostering research that contributes to national development. The university emphasizes both theoretical knowledge and practical skills, preparing students to meet the challenges of the modern world.
